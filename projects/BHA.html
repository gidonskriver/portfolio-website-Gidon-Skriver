Graphic Design

Blue Horizon Airways

A logo design concept for a fictional airline brand, built around a clean, professional and trustworthy appearance.

Year

2024

Category

Graphic Design

Focus

Logo Development, Business Card, Brand Concept

Overview

Blue Horizon Airways was created in the Graphic Design module. The assignment was to design a business card which includes a logo for a fictional brand of our choice. It was important to try out many different approaches so that the final design would be the result of a thorough exploration process.

Approach

I chose to create a logo for an airline brand, because I had multiple ideas for how to visually represent such a company. After many iterations, I decided to go with a head of a falcon as the main logo element, to commuinicate the qualities of speed, precision and reliability. The color palette I used, is strongly inspired by the colors of the sky and air.
