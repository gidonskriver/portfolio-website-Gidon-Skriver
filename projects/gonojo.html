Crossmedia Campaign

GonoJo

A crossmedia brand and campaign concept for a Berlin iced tea start-up, created to position a sugar- and preservative-free product in a way that feels energetic, natural and clearly distinct from the market.

Year

2025

Category

Brand Identity / Campaign

Focus

Packaging, Poster Design, Social Media Concept, Webdesign

Overview

GonoJo is a fictional Berlin start-up founded by two friends who met during their sports studies and bonded over electronic music. Inspired by festival culture, they developed iced teas made from caffeinated black tea and organically grown Berlin berries to offer a refreshing and naturally energizing alternative for young, health-conscious people between 15 and 30. For the planned summer 2025 launch, the brand needed a visual identity and communication concept that could introduce the products to a wider audience and build a strong community.

Approach

My approach was to translate the brand story into a crossmedia identity that feels youthful, fresh and distinctive. To support this, I introduced two recurring brand icons, the lightning bolt and the droplet, which represent the product’s core qualities: energy and refreshment. The visual identity is built around a natural color palette inspired by the ingredients and combined with youthful, urban influences. The communication concept focuses on user-centered content to build a strong and engaged community around the brand.
