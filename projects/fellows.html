Corporate Publishing

The Fellows Companion

An editorial magazine and social media concept for a real coffee brand, combining long-form storytelling with a warm and approachable visual identity.

Year

2024

Category

Editorial / Social Media Concept

Focus

Magazine Layout, Cover Story, Content Concept

Overview

This project was created in the Corporate Publishing module. The assignment was to develop a customer magazine, write the cover story and build a bridge between the customer magazine and the brand’s social media channels through a consistent editorial concept. Note: The magazine and social media concept are fictional and were created solely for the purpose of this assignment.

Approach

I chose Coffee Fellows because I already knew the brand from a local café near my home and wanted to build the project around a place with a familiar atmosphere and a clear identity. The editorial concept centers on the founding story of the brand, which became the cover story, and extends into social media through community-driven ideas such as user-generated content and audience participation (#FellowMoments, which is a foto competition, in which users submit their best moments with the brand). Visually, the design is based on the existing brand palette and the layout was refined over several iterations to become more open, image-led and easier to read.
